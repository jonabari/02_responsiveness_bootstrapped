About Me

The first time I stole so that I wouldn't starve. I lost many assumptions about the simple nature of right and wrong. And when I traveled I learned the fear before a crime and the thrill of success. But I never became one of them. I am not one of them.

A hero can be anyone. Even a man knowing something as simple and reassuring as putting a coat around a young boy's shoulders to let him know the world hadn't ended. That's a hero. I want to be more.

But I can't do that as Bruce Wayne... as a man. I'm flesh and blood. I can be ignored,destroyed. But as a symbol, I can be incorruptible, I can be everlasting.
